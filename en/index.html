Benefits of the ABV platform

ABV is the only digital platform in Uzbekistan with which you can conduct business. All business services are assembled into a single system with a user-friendly interface. Painstaking work procedures with accounting reports, taxes and personnel accounting are carried out automatically in the shortest possible time.

The automated system will simplify and speed up the registration of your business in Uzbekistan. Regardless of the chosen form of ownership and field of activity, you are guaranteed to get the best result in the shortest possible time.

Professional documentation in a few clicks

The Document Constructor is an ideal addition for any company that seeks to save time and money on creating documentation. Our templates guarantee that you will always have a legally competent document for any transaction.

Electronic
document management

Legal force and no paper: fast and easy

The system allows you to exchange electronic documents in real time with counterparties across the country. The service reduces costs and speeds up document flow: the exchange of any types of documents takes place inside the system without duplication on paper.

Working with a Bank

Full access to current account data and statements

The service "Working with a Bank" allows you to send payment documents to the bank and receive extracts from it by pressing one button.

Human Resources

Calculation of wages, vacation, sick leave and other payments — with one click

If you have a staff of employees, you cannot do without proper personnel accounting and record keeping. This will reduce the risks of violations of labor legislation, fines and court costs.

Warehouse

Automatic warehouse accounting, 24/7 control of the movement of goods

"Warehouse" is an ideal tool for inventory management, shipment and receipt. Work with labeling, get sales statistics. Out-of-stock notifications will remind you to re-order. The service is easily integrated and scaled — optimize your business.

Accounting

Automated and online — focus on business

"Accounting" is ideal for running and tracking your business. The system will allow you to easily manage finances, issue invoices, track expenses and freely collect tax reports.
